#### description
[[Finara]] was a hired assassin who, one day, accepted a contract from the son of a wealthy merchant, [[Finley Ouroden]]. He sought to claim his inheritance as quickly as possible by arranging the murder of his father, [[Albert Ouroden]].

At the time Finara accepted the contract, Albert was already traveling through the northern pass toward eastern Mundos, with plans to return by ship. Finara needed to find a faster route to reach the eastern side before he departed.

Believing that a shortcut must exist through the mountains of [[south_mundos]], she searched relentlessly, traveling day and night without rest. She explored nearly every cave within the [[Trévilské hory]] until she eventually discovered a long passage leading directly to eastern Mundos, emerging in an abandoned cave that allowed her to reach her target in time.

She successfully carried out the assassination. Initially, the blame fell on eastern bandits, but Finara later revealed both the existence of the passage and her role in Albert’s death. After that, she disappeared and was never seen again.

During later expeditions through [[Finařina stezka]], an entrance to the [[Trévilské Výšiny]] was also discovered.
