A village caught between past and present, where the player experiences a repeating massacre through shifting timelines.

## Overview
An abandoned village trapped between past and present, where players experience shifting timelines during combat. The location is tied to the main quest (disappearances and isolation of the region).

## Narrative Role
- Explains supernatural events in the region
- Provides a contained story (Lady Alison & Sir Kaelen)
- Acts as a potential solution path (magical reward / communication)

## Core Mechanic (Time Shift)
The environment alternates between two states:
- **Past (massacre)** - undead enemies
- **Present (ruins)** - blights and overgrown nature
	Each round:
- Random shift between past and present
- Only one enemy group active at a time
- Inactive enemies are “paused” but persist

## Layout
- Entrance path (visual decay, foreshadowing)
- Village center (main combat area)
- Scattered houses (optional exploration / rewards)
- Alison’s house (resolution point)

## Gameplay Intent
- Unpredictable combat flow (state switching)
- Environmental storytelling (ruins vs living memory)
- Environment alternates between past and present
- Exploration vs risk (loot vs danger)

## Player Experience
- Confusion → understanding → resolution
- Gradual reveal of the story through environment
- Emotional payoff (lifting the curse)

## Rewards
- Magical scrolls (optional)
- Weapon (+1 weapon)
- Blessing or communication spell (Sending)

## Notes
- Designed as a self-contained narrative dungeon
- Focus on atmosphere over difficulty scaling
