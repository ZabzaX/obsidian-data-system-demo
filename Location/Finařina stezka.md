#### description
Travel through [[Finařina stezka]] from [[western_mundos]] to [[eastern_mundos]] typically takes around 3 to 4 days. If caravans stop in the Trévil Highlands, the journey is extended by approximately 2 additional days, and even longer if they spend time in [[Harven]].

In total, the journey can take up to a full week before reaching the other side.
